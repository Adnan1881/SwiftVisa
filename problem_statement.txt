People often struggle to understand visa eligibility requirements because immigration policies are lengthy, technical, and written in legal language that is difficult for normal users to interpret. Users ask questions in natural language, while official guidelines use formal terminology, making traditional keyword search or rule-based systems unreliable. Therefore, there is a need for an intelligent system that can understand user queries, retrieve the most relevant visa policies from official sources, and later evaluate eligibility based on those policies. The SwiftVisa project addresses this by building an AI-based semantic retrieval and reasoning agent that converts visa rules into machine-understandable knowledge and assists users in understanding their visa eligibility accurately.
